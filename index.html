About me

I began my career at McDonald's as a crew member. Subsequently, I spent four years working in a chat service as a chat specialist, providing customer service in the USA. There was always a spark within me to do something more, and that led me to start learning frontend web development alongside my role as a chat specialist.

I enrolled in a comprehensive meta frontend web development course on Coursera, covering HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, React, UI/UX principles, and version control. Following this, I pursued an internship at Vitsol. Post-internship, I dedicated time to improving my skills further, working from home on my laptop.

Eventually, I made the decision to resign from my position as a chat specialist and took on my first job at NayyarTech in 2023. Simultaneously, I undertook several freelancing projects, applying my skills and gaining valuable experience.
